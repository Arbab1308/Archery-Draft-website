COMMON QUESTIONS

HOW LONG IS THE CURRENT WAITLIST?
+

Our current waitlist for a bespoke IMPERATOR bow is approximately 6 to 8 months. Each bow is handcrafted by a single master bowyer from start to finish, ensuring unparalleled quality that cannot be rushed.

WHAT KIND OF WOOD DO YOU USE?
+

We primarily utilize sustainably sourced Dark Walnut, White Oak, and Exotic Figured Maples. Every piece is hand-selected for its structural integrity, grain density, and aesthetic character.

ARE THE BOWS CUSTOMIZABLE?
+

Yes. Every IMPERATOR bow is an investment tailored to the individual. You can specify draw weight, length, wood combination, and custom engravings during the consultation phase.

WHAT IS THE WARRANTY?
+

We provide a Lifetime Craftsmanship Warranty. If any structural failure occurs due to a defect in materials or workmanship, we will repair or replace your bow at no cost.
